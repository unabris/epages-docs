Product Relations

Definition

A product relation is a reference from a product entity to another resource.

Characteristics

  • Can be used to extend product basic attributes with additional information.
  • Consists of a relation name (name) and a URI (href).
  • A product can have any number of product relations, but names must be unique.
  • Product relations can't be updated (yet).
